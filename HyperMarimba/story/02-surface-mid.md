After choosing $(X, \Gamma)$, pressing _Run geodesic and save musical data_ will select a random initial vector and calculate the sequence of hits for the given length. Everytime you press this button a different random vector is chosen, leading to a different _melody_.

In the two subpanels below, one can visualize the geodesic in a hexagon decomposition corresponding to the given pants decomposition, read the _score_ of the produced _melody_, and listen to it.
