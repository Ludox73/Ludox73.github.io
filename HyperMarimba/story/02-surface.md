In this visualization we only consider surfaces of genus 2. These are described in terms of Fenchel-Nielsen coordinates, which can be adjusted below (for computational reasons, the length of the curves are constrained in some limits).

The multicurve $\Gamma$ can be selected by first choosing the combinatorics of the pair-of-pants decomposition and then a subcollection of its components. Notice that all possible topological configurations can be obtained in this way.
